elysium
Towering steel built next to low-rise buildings, Noctem is the closest New Manhattan gets to the concrete jungles of old. Due to how the filter systems of the city are positioned, the sector has never gotten the cleanest air. It’s still been declared as livable without personal filtration gear, but long term residents know this isn’t quite true, with the edges of the sector being particular hotspots for respiratory issues. At the same time, this paired with its antique look, makes it one of the most affordable places to live. What it lacks in glitter and glam, it makes up with the history that seems to live in the very bones of Noctem.
